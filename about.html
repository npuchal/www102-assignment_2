It starts with a question:
“What do you want to be when you grow up?”

As a kid, there wasn’t one specific word that defined a person that wanted to take part in all things that were creative. There was musician, painter, writer, but never that one, all-encompassing word to define what is now “a creative.”

Hi, I’m Natalie Puchalski – a 22-year-old multidisciplinary creative, who is passionate about all things involving design or creation. I particularly love graphic & web design, writing, singing & songwriting, video game marketing, and video editing. Simply put, I'm a bit of a jack-of-all-trades, whose core focus leans on creation.

I’m currently finishing my undergraduate degree at the Schulich School of Business, pursuing an International Bachelor of Business Administration with a specialization in Marketing, and now completing a 14-week Webmaster course at Seneca College, but to say that I am just a business or web design student would be a mistake.

I am first and foremost a firm believer in cohesion and unity across a brand. Having worked with local bands to multi-billion dollar companies on their branding, my focus is always on developing tools and materials that reflect your brand's core values and beliefs. With knowledge that spans across industries and trades, I can help bring your vision to life, no matter the type.

Apart from being passionate about creative endeavours, you can also find me going on hikes with my dog, playing videos both old and new, and travelling the world, one country at a time - next stop: Indonesia!

Need to get a hold of me? Feel free to email me at npuchal@gmail.com.
